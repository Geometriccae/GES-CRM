About Us

Mahalashmi Women's College of Arts and Science, was established in the year 1998 with the mission to impart higher education to the women students and to bring about a holistic development in their career. Thiru. S. Kunjithapatham, the Chairman of our institution is highly passionate about providing quality education to empower women who are from semi- urban vicinity. The institution promises value embedded education with social responsibility.

Our Courses

Library- All students, faculty members of the Institute are entitled to make use of the Library facilities on taking library membership. The Library, besides having a huge collection of books on Arts & science offers library services through its various Divisions. Library serves as a resource center and aims to develop a comprehensive collection of documents useful for Faculty members and students of the institute and provides an efficient dissemination of knowledge.

The Library is to engage the learning and discovery as essential participants in the educational community. We develop, organize, provide access to and preserve materials to meet the needs of present and future generations of students and scholars. Library intends to incorporate the latest technology and adopts user friendly approach towards students and Faculty.

Placement cell is one of the most important and distinguished department that has built its brand among the corporate sector, delivering the most enigmatic and spirited youngsters to the corporate world.

This cell also coordinates various activities related to the career growth of the students by conducting programs on “Personality development”, “Interpersonal & communication skills” and “Career planning” and “career mapping”. A consistent placement record has been maintained since its inception, 90% of Eligible Candidates are placed consecutively in several multinational companies offering job opportunities for students in various sectors like, Information Technology, Banking and Financial, Life Science, Manufacturing, Advertising agencies, BPOs, KPOs, NGOs etc.
